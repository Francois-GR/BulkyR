BulkyR

Trade with your fellow Neighbors, score the perfect material for your next project or get rid of the project material that is collecting dust

  • take a taxt break when you and your neighborhood start using BulkyR

  • Design the container's shape and aesthetics yourself

  • walk through cleaner streets

  • Legally dispose of clutter such as old sofas and tables

  • create a profile and always know what there is to find before looking

  • earn rewards with the app

For people who have old furniture they no longer need and for people who could still use old furniture, BulkyR is the perfect solution. It offers kind of shelters where locals can come and bring their old furniture they don't need anymore and other locals can come and see if they could still use the furniture. In a time where vintage is a trend, it is very interesting to work this way and there is less waste and no more dirty streets.
